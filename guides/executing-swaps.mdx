# Executing Swaps

This guide will walk you through the lifecycle of a swap on Hyperion.

## Steps

1.  **Get a Quote**: Query the `/quote` endpoint.
2.  **Build Transaction**: Send the quote to `/swap`.
3.  **Sign**: Sign the returned transaction (either with your wallet or a Session Key).
4.  **Submit**: Broadcast to the network.
